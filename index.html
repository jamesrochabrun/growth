A calculated marketing strategy can drive sales, increase market presence and enhance your brand's overall value to customers. That is why, together with industry experts, you will plan out a growth hacking strategy for the next three months that will deliver results.

In this workshop you will receive:

  • A framework & plan that can be implemented immediately
  • An analysis of your current marketing strategy
  • In depth summary and recommendations from industry experts
  • A checklist that you can keep at your desk to keep you focused and moving forward

Starting 10 AM, you will be learning key growth strategies and building out your plan. By 5 PM you and your team will have a three month growth plan ready to implement in your business. Three months later we will meet again and review your progress.
